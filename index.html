§ 1. OVERVIEW

Research Vision

I am a graduate researcher actively pursuing a Master of Science in Applied Mathematics and Computational Science. My work lies at the intersection of theoretical fluid dynamics, computational applications, and machine learning, incorporating advanced numerical methods to gain new insights into multiphysics modeling and bridging the gap between theory and engineering applications.

I am currently working on multiple projects including mixed convective heat transfer, neural ordinary differential equations, and data-driven equation discovery. My fundamental academic vision is to pioneer the field of quantum computing in Bangladesh, fostering a collaborative scientific community through rigorous research and meaningful collaborations.
