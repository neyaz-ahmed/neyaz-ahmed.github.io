§ 1. PROFILE

Biography

[Image Placeholder]

Hello, I am Neyaz Ahmed. I am currently pursuing a Master of Science in Applied Mathematics and Computational Science at North South University (GPA: 3.51), where I serve as a Graduate Research Assistant and a member of the Big Matrix Lab.

My academic foundation began with a B.S. in Petroleum and Mining Engineering from Shahjalal University of Science and Technology (SUST). Since then, my research has evolved strictly into the domains of theoretical fluid dynamics, multiphysics modeling, and high-performance scientific computing.

I am particularly passionate about integrating Machine Learning into physical systems—utilizing Physics-Informed Neural Networks (PINNs) and Neural ODEs. My long-term academic aim is rigorous: I intend to pioneer the field of quantum computing in Bangladesh through sustained, data-driven mathematical research.
