§ 1. The Brinkman-Forchheimer Model

In porous media simulations, the momentum equation often incorporates the Forchheimer term to account for high-velocity inertial effects. However, numerical singularities arise when the velocity field $u$ approaches zero.

Note: The term $(u^2 + v^2)^{-0.5}$ in the Jacobian calculation is the primary source of the "Negative power of zero" error. Regularization via a small epsilon constant is recommended.
