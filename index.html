[Image Placeholder]

I am a math enthusiast and a graduate researcher at North South University. My academic background is rooted in engineering, but my primary focus and specialty now lie heavily in linear algebra and partial differential equations.

Currently, my thesis work centers on Computational Fluid Dynamics (CFD), specifically investigating transport phenomena within porous media and nanoparticles. Alongside my fluid research, I am actively working on applying machine learning to physical systems, building predictive models, and exploring neural differential equations.

Looking ahead, my long-term goal is to explore topological data analysis and differential topology—investigating how spatial and geometric properties can provide robust, alternative ways to model and solve complex dynamical systems.
