§ 1. About

Introduction

[Photo]

I am a math enthusiast and a graduate researcher at North South University. My academic background is rooted in engineering, but my primary focus and specialty now lie heavily in linear algebra and partial differential equations.

Currently, my thesis work centres on Computational Fluid Dynamics (CFD), specifically investigating transport phenomena within porous media and nanoparticles. Alongside my fluid research, I am actively applying machine learning to physical systems — building predictive models and exploring neural differential equations.

My long-term goal is to explore topological data analysis and differential topology, investigating how spatial and geometric properties can provide robust, alternative ways to model and solve complex dynamical systems.
